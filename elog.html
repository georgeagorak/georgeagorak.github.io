03-08-2024 Majority Snowdon 2 'Lucky' Repair

So this has been a weird repair.

I have recived this Majority Snowdon 2 soundbar from a friend of mine as he would it to be faulty.
The problem he said was that 'as if the volume down button is constently pressed'. So I got interested.
The first thing I have done was tested that the speaker was faulty at all! Which weirdly the soundbar did work... but not for long.

I have found that the speak would indeed keep pressing the volume button, I checked the button but it seemed fine. I was considering the remote and RF sensor, I replaced the RF receiver as it was a standard ones and I had some lying around. That did not fixed the fault unfortunately.

I have started checking the manual button output.

I have found that the buttons result in the different voltage drop.
I have left this repair for months and meanwhile I have lost its power supply ;). So I have ordered a new one.

I have started to test it again for a prelong time to replcate the fault while I got some probes on it, however it seems that it has fixed be itself!!!!!

Of course, I do not believe it so my educated guess would be that the previous power supply was fault and somehow was dropping voltage on switches enough to tigger the sound down action.
